Our Services

Residential & Custom Homes

Colton Construction Company takes pride in bringing your home to life. We will guide you all the way through the building process. With our knowledge and experience, you can rest assured everything will be taken care of and your beautiful home will be all you dreamed it would be!

Oregon Parks & Trails

We love the outdoors and enjoy getting creative (and dirty!) building parks and bike trails all over Oregon. Our work includes the Cazadero Bike Trails, Banks Vernonia Bike Trail, Cottonwood Canyon Visitor Center, Umatilla Gun Range and briefing building, Aurora City Park, Magnolia Park, Metzger Park, Tryon Creek, Milo McIver Park, and Latourell Falls — just to name a few.

Commercial Projects

After the housing market fell in 2008, we successfully pivoted into state-level work. We've gained experience on diverse projects like the Expo Center GreenWall, Pfriem Family Brewers (original build and addition), Rugg Road Landslide Repair, River Rd & Park Ave Pedestrian Improvements, and various remodels and repairs at the Oregon Metro Zoo.
